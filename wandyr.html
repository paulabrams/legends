The Wilderland

Wåndyr expects to start in an undefined "wilderland" that emerges during play. You can optionally place that somewhere in the fantasy world of your choice, or use the Dragon Isles, below.

Old-School Roleplaying

Wåndyr is compatible with "old school" roleplaying games, so you can use traditional equipment lists, monsters, and modules. You can use any resources you like, but the below from are highly recommended.
